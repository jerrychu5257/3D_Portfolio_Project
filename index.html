About QUAD

夸果設計代表了跨域創作的一種精神，背景橫跨了工程、3D動畫、演唱會、光雕展演與沈浸式視覺設計，以及互動體驗設計等等。夸果由四個虛擬角色組合在一起，依次為“理性”、“熱情”、“實驗精神”、“好奇心”，專注於設計讓人回味無窮的視覺互動體驗。

QUAD represents a spirit of cross-disciplinary creation, with backgrounds spanning engineering, 3D animation, concerts, projection mapping performances, immersive visual design, and interactive experience design, etc. QUAD is composed of four virtual characters, namely "rationality", "passion", "experimental spirit" and "curiosity". It focuses on designing an unforgettable visual interactive experience.
